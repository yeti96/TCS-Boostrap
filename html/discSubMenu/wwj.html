WORLD WAR JOY

World War Joy is a studio album by the American duo The Chainsmokers . It was launched on 6 of December of 2019 distributed through Disruptor and Columbia Records, with collaborations like Illenium, Lennon Stella, Bebe Rexha , Ty Dolla $ ign, Bülow, 5 Seconds of Summer , blink-182 , Sabrina Claudio , KYGO and Amy Shark!
